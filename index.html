About Me



I am currently a Consultant with the Market Access division of Two Labs Pharma Services (now part of Envision Pharma Group). For the past 2 years my role has been focused around strategic market access consulting for pharmaceutical and biotech manufacturers, primarily pricing. payer strategy, value message development/testing and new product launch planning. I have worked on US and global projects involving key European and Asian markets, with experience in the oncology, asthma, migraine, and immunology therapeutic areas, as well as diagnostic testing and digital therapeutics.

I began teaching myself to code in Python at the beginning of 2020 as the COVID-19 pandemic was kicking off. I took a couple of CS courses while at UCSD, but had not revisited programming (beyond Excel modeling) until last year. As I delved deeper into the world of CS my passion for coding grew, and I have since begun learning other skills such as SQL, HTML/CSS/JavaScript, the Django and Bootstrap web frameworks, and Python data science packages for analytics and machine learning. See the 'Projects' section of my site to see some of my work so far. I have also applied my learnings to my current role, introducing efficiencies through automation of some traditional project tasks.

Originally from Trenton, NJ, I grew up in Tracy, CA until heading off to college at UC San Diego in 2012. Since graduating college, I have resided in beautiful, sunny San Diego, currently in the North Park neighborhood. You can likely find me working at a local coffee shop or brewery (Mike Hess and Second Chance are some of my favorites). Interestingly, my first 2 employers both had offices in the San Diego Union Tribune building (seen in the foreground).
